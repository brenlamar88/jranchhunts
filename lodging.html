J Ranch/Lodge & Dining

Come in from the field

A working-ranch lodge built for comfort — good beds, better food, and a porch that earns its keep at the end of a long day.

The Lodge

Rough country, easy nights

The main lodge sleeps groups in private and shared rooms, all built around a great room with a limestone fireplace and a table long enough for the whole camp. It's comfortable without pretending to be a hotel — because it isn't one.

Wi-Fi where you want it, quiet where you need it, and a porch that looks straight down the canyon for the part of the evening that isn't about hunting at all.

The great room porch

Dining

Nobody leaves hungry

Three squares a day, cooked on-site. Breakfast before the morning hunt, a sit-down dinner every night, and a sack lunch in the field when the hunt runs long. Tell us about allergies or a favorite cut and we'll take care of it.

  • Hearty pre-dawn breakfasts and hot camp coffee
  • Wild game, mesquite-grilled steaks, and Gulf seafood dinners
  • Field lunches packed for long days
  • BYOB — we'll keep it cold
