Exotic Hunts

No season, no limit, no fence-line feel

Exotics don't follow a Texas season, so J Ranch hunts them year-round — which makes for a perfect summer or off-season trip. Our animals range free across the whole property, so every hunt is a real spot-and-stalk or blind hunt, not a shooting pen.

Most exotic hunts are all-inclusive, three days and two nights, and you can combine species on one trip. Take an axis and a blackbuck in the same weekend, or add an exotic to a whitetail hunt in the fall.

  • Hunted year-round — ideal spring and summer trips
  • Rifle, bow, crossbow, pistol, and blackpowder welcome
  • Combo any two or more species on a single stay
