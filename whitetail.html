Managed trophy whitetail

A quarter-century of genetics work and habitat management, hunted in small groups over some of the best country in the Hill Country.

The Program

Built buck by buck

Our whitetail herd is the product of 25 seasons of selective harvest, supplemental nutrition, and careful genetics. The result is consistent access to mature, heavy-horned deer across a range of score classes.

Most whitetail hunts run three days and two nights, all-inclusive. You'll hunt mornings and evenings with your guide and spend the middle of the day at the lodge or on the range. Rut hunts book first — reserve early.

  • Season generally runs November through January
  • Mornings and evenings guided; scout-and-stalk available
  • Field dressing, caping, and cold storage included
