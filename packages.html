Good to Know

Booking terms

What's the deposit?

A 50% non-refundable deposit reserves your dates. The remaining balance is due before departure.

How do trophy fees work?

Trophy fees are confirmed before the shot, so there are never surprises. Availability is subject to seasonal conditions, and prices may change without notice.

What should I bring?

Your weapon and ammo, weather-appropriate hunting clothes, boots, and personal items. We handle field care, cold storage, and everything at the ranch — a full checklist goes out with your confirmation.

Do you charge for wounded animals?

Wounded animals are considered harvested. Your guide will do everything possible to recover the animal, and we ask every hunter to take only ethical, well-placed shots.
