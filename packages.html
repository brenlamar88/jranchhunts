Good to Know

Booking terms

What's the deposit?

A 50% deposit confirms your dates. The remaining balance is due on arrival by cash, check, or card. Deposits are transferable to another date within the same season with two weeks' notice.

What licenses do I need?

Texas requires a valid hunting license for native game like whitetail and turkey. Exotics can be hunted year-round with no license required in Texas. We'll walk you through exactly what you need when you book.

What should I bring?

Your weapon and ammo, weather-appropriate hunting clothes, boots, a valid license where required, and personal items. We provide blinds, dogs, field gear, game care, and everything at the lodge. A full checklist goes out with your confirmation.

Do you charge for wounded animals?

Yes — a wounded-and-lost animal is charged as harvested. Your guide will do everything possible to recover, and we ask every hunter to only take ethical, well-placed shots.
