Welcome to J Ranch

The country makes the hunt

Tucked into a fold of the Hill Country where the caliche gives way to live oak and juniper, J Ranch has been in one family since 1997. We run it the way we'd want to be hosted — small groups, honest game, and a guide who's hunted this ground his whole life.

Two hundred feet of elevation change, a half-dozen spring-fed tanks, and food plots worked year-round make for country that holds animals and rewards the patient hunter. What you take home is a trophy. What you keep is the morning it happened.

  • Free-ranging whitetail and exotics on managed range
  • One guide for every two hunters — never a crowd
  • All-inclusive lodging, meals, field care, and trophy prep

Hunt hard. Rest easy.
